# Context& Brand Guidelines

Apply these standards whenever producing Context&-branded materials (decks, documents, emails, web copy, social posts).

## Brand Essence

- **Tone**: human and professional, yet still techy — sleek and approachable
- **Tagline**: *"Better connected in The Digital Neighbourhood"*
- **2030 Aspiration**: Recognised as a Microsoft Powerhouse
- **Winning Behaviours**: Uncompromising Quality · Insane Customer Focus · Excellent Employee Experience · Bold Innovation

## Logo Rules

- The logotype must **never** appear without the **&** (ampersand) — the ampersand is the brand icon.
- **Two-coloured logo** (black wordmark + purple ampersand) → use on white or light grey backgrounds.
- **One-coloured logo** (white) → use on coloured or dark backgrounds.
- Never place the logo over a person's face or chest-up portrait.
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the ampersand.

## Typography

**Primary: Aktiv Grotesk** (fallback: Neue Haas Grotesk Text Pro Roman)
**Secondary: PP Neue Machina Plain Regular** (fallback: Neue Haas Grotesk Text Pro Bold) — used sparingly, ~30% of typography mix.

| Use | Weight | Line height | Letter spacing |
|-----|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Headlines | Light | 130% | 0% |
| Sub-header / body | Light | 145% | 0% |
| Box text | Regular | 160% | 0% |
| Buttons | Medium | 130% | 5% |
| Small text | Regular | 160% | 0% |
| Text links | Medium | 160% | 5% |
| Tags & markers | PP Neue Machina, UPPERCASE | — | 20% |

PP Neue Machina is for: highlighted keywords in big titles, highlighted numbers, quotes (in purple `#5500FF`), short alternate titles, and tags/markers.

## Iconography

- **Stroke icons** in purple `#5500FF`, soft/rounded corners.
- **1.5pt** stroke for service icons; **1pt** stroke for resource icons.
- Business-area icons may use the gradient palette above.

## Business Areas

CRM Sales · CRM Marketing · AI Agents · Customer Service & Support · Business Continuity · Data & Insights · ERP · Web & E-Commerce · Modern Work · Projectum xPM · Custom Solutions

## Photography

- Urban, cool, authentic — real people, friendly and casual.
- Natural warm light; city / architectural backgrounds preferred.
- **Do not** apply the legacy 20% black tint overlay.
- **Do not** place the logo on top of a person from chest up.

## Layout & Framing

- **Rounded corners**: 40px on desktop, 30px on mobile.
- Portrait photos: fully rounded (circle).
- Viva Engage assets: always circular crops.
- Graphic elements: purple shapes (semicircles, circles, arcs) — derived from the curve of the **c** in the ampersand.

## When NOT to Use

- Personal notes, drafts, or scratch work that won't be shared
- Non-Context& deliverables (client templates, partner-branded materials)
- Internal-only quick replies where formatting adds no value

## How to Apply

1. When generating a document, deck, or email, default to: white background, black body text, Aktiv Grotesk Light for headlines.
2. Use purple `#5500FF` only for CTAs, the ampersand, quotes, or small accents — never as a body-text colour.
3. If a deck cover or hero is needed, suggest a purple semicircle or arc graphic and the tagline.
4. For headlines mixing fonts, use Aktiv Grotesk Light with a single PP Neue Machina keyword for emphasis.
5. When inserting placeholder imagery, describe the photography style (urban, real people, warm natural light) rather than inserting generic stock.
6. End external communications with the tagline where appropriate: *"Better connected in The Digital Neighbourhood."*
